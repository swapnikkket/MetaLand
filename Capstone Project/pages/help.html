Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center

Here you will find all the information you need to navigate and enjoy the 2D MetaLand.

Getting Started

How to Create an Account

1. Click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage or here.

2. Fill in your details such as username, email, and password.

3. Click 'Submit' to create your account.

How to Log In

1. Click on the 'Login' button on the homepage or here.

2. Enter your email and password.

3. Click 'Login' to access your account.

Basic Navigation and Interface Overview

Once logged in, you will see the navigation menu at the top of the screen. This menu allows you to access different areas of the MetaLand including the Social Hub, Games, Events, Shops, and more.

Profile Management

Updating Profile Information

1. Go to your profile page by clicking 'Profile' in the navigation menu.

2. Click 'Edit Profile'.

3. Update your information such as your username, bio, and profile picture.

4. Click 'Save' to apply the changes.

Customizing Avatars

1. Navigate to your profile page.

2. Click 'Customize Avatar'.

3. Choose from various customization options such as hairstyles, outfits, and accessories.

4. Click 'Save' to update your avatar.

Privacy Settings

1. Go to your profile page and click 'Privacy Settings'.

2. Adjust your settings to control who can see your profile and interact with you.

3. Click 'Save' to apply your changes.

Key Areas

The MetaLand is divided into several key areas:

  • Social Hub: Meet, chat, and interact with other users.
  • Games: Play a variety of games with friends and other users.
  • Events: Participate in virtual events, concerts, and workshops.
  • Shops: Browse and purchase virtual items from different categories.

Interacting with Others

Adding Friends

1. Navigate to a user's profile by clicking their name or searching for them.

2. Click 'Add Friend'.

3. Once they accept your request, you will be connected as friends.

Messaging and Communication

1. Click on the 'Messages' icon in the navigation menu.

2. Select a friend from your list or search for a user to start a conversation.

3. Type your message and hit 'Send'.

Participating in Events

1. Go to the 'Events' section from the navigation menu.

2. Browse the list of upcoming events and click on the ones you're interested in.

3. Click 'RSVP' to confirm your attendance.

Shops and Purchases

Browsing Shops

1. Click on 'Shops' in the navigation menu.

2. Choose a category such as Grocery, Electronics, Fashion, Beauty, Home & Garden, or Sports & Outdoors.

3. Browse through the items available in each category.

Making Purchases

1. Select an item you wish to purchase.

2. Click 'Add to Cart'.

3. Go to your cart by clicking the cart icon.

4. Review your items and click 'Checkout'.

5. Enter your payment details and click 'Confirm Purchase'.

Managing Your Inventory

1. Go to your profile and click 'Inventory'.

2. View all the items you have purchased.

3. Click on an item to use or manage it.

Jobs and Freelance

Finding and Applying for Jobs

1. Navigate to the 'Jobs/Freelance' section from the navigation menu.

2. Browse the list of available jobs.

3. Click on a job that interests you to view the details.

4. Click 'Apply' and follow the instructions to submit your application.

Offering Freelance Services

1. Go to the 'Jobs/Freelance' section.

2. Click 'Offer Services'.

3. Fill in the details about the service you are offering.

4. Click 'Submit' to post your service.

Payment and Transactions

1. Payments for jobs and freelance services are handled securely within the platform.

2. Ensure your payment details are up to date in your profile settings.

3. You will be notified when you receive payments for your services.

2. Browse the list of upcoming events.

3. Click on an event to view the details.

Hosting Your Own Events
